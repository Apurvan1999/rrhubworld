Skeepers gives you a team of experts dedicated to your success.

We work with more than 8,000 brands

3 expert divisions at your service

At Skeepers, we are committed to your ongoing success. Every day we strive to ensure you get the maximum value from our SaaS solution suite. We have three expert divisions at your service:

  • Customer Success Managers
  • Professional Services
  • Customer Care

Customer Success Managers

Our Customer Success Managers are committed to ensuring you get maximum value from our solutions. As a Skeepers client, you have your own dedicated Customer Success Manager. These business experts have a strong understand of your industry and seasoned experience in advising clients on running successful campaigns.

Your Customer Success Manager will:

  • Help you identify your challenges, meet your needs from the outset and make proactive suggestions to optimize your campaign success.

 

  • Organize steering committees to ensure your project is on track, review your KPIs and provide you with tips and advice.

 

  • Present new features and share insights on future roadmap functionality.

 

  • Invite you to take part in workshops and events to help you optimize your strategy and meet your peers.

 

  • Share exclusive content, such as research, white papers, case studies on specific areas of interest and other helpful content

Professional Services

Our Professional Services team are specialists in the technical and functional implementation of all aspects of our solution suite. Just like our Customer Success Managers, they are business experts, but bring an added layer of technical insight. We understand that every project is unique, which is why we adapt our services to your needs and objectives.

 

We even offer a service to use our dedicated Campaign and Project Managers to oversee the operational side of your campaigns and manage your day-to-day tasks and new projects around specific projects.

Our Professional Services team will:

  • Help you unlock the full potential and rich functionality of the Skeepers solution suite, share our methodology so you can leverage the full potential of Skeepers and use our SaaS solutions autonomously from day one.

 

  • Share best practices, set up specific training sessions with your team and share user guides tailored to your key challenges and objectives.

Customer Care

At Skeepers, one of the most important parts of our organization is Customer Care to ensure you have help on hand when using our technology.

Our dedicated Customer Care team will:

  • Answer any questions and find the best solution to a query as quickly as possible.

 

  • Provide documentation on all functional aspects of the Skeepers solution suite.

 

  • Provide you with regular updates.

 

  • Share all your precious feedback with our relevant internal departments as part of our ethos on continuous improvement.
